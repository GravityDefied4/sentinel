See Through the Government with SILIP

Project Streamlined Information on Local Inundation Prevention (SILIP) is a web-based transparency portal that consolidates and visualizes data related to flood control and mitigation projects. The platform provides an interactive interface where users can explore project locations, funding details, implementation status, and associated government officials.

About Us

SENTINEL is a group of Information Technology students dedicated to creating technology-driven solutions that promote transparency, accessibility, and public awareness. As the developers of Project SILIP, the team aims to bridge the gap between government data and the Filipino public by transforming complex flood control information into a clear, interactive, and user-friendly platform. Through innovation and accountability-focused design, SENTINEL strives to empower communities with accessible information that encourages informed civic participation and disaster awareness.

Features

Discover the powerful features of SILIP designed to enhance your flood prevention planning.

1

Project Monitoring Dashboard

Shows latest data on budget allocation and timelines of existing projects.

2

Geographic Data Integration

Uses standardized location codes to accurately map projects to specific administrative areas.

3

Interactive Map Visualization

Displays flood control projects geographically using an interactive map, allowing users to zoom into specific regions, provinces, cities, and barangays.

4

Search and Filter System

Allows users to filter projects by region and province.

5

Data Transparency Layer

Presents raw and processed data in an accessible format for public viewing and analysis.
